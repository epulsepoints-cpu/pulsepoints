‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start learning?

Tap the "Learn" tab and begin with Module 1: ECG Basics. Each module builds on the previous one, so follow the sequence for best results.

What are hearts and how do I get them?

Hearts represent your learning energy. You start with 5 hearts daily. Correct answers maintain hearts, while mistakes cost hearts. Hearts refill automatically over time.

How does the streak system work?

Maintain your streak by completing at least one lesson daily. Longer streaks unlock achievements and show your dedication to learning ECG interpretation.

Can I use this app offline?

Basic functionality works offline, but you'll need internet connection for progress sync, new content updates, and authentication features.

Is this app a substitute for medical education?

No. ECGkid is a supplementary learning tool. Always consult qualified medical professionals and official curricula for medical education and patient care decisions.

How do I reset my progress?

Go to App Settings ‚Üí Clear All App Data. This will reset your progress and settings. This action cannot be undone.

Why can't I access certain lessons?

Lessons unlock progressively as you complete previous ones. This ensures you build knowledge systematically. Complete earlier lessons to unlock advanced content.
